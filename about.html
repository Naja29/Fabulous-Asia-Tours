About Fabulous Asia Tours

Your Trusted Partner for Unforgettable Sri Lankan Adventures

Our Story

Founded with a passion for showcasing the beauty and culture of Sri Lanka, Fabulous Asia Tours has grown to become one of the island's most trusted and respected travel service providers. Our journey began with a simple vision: to create authentic, memorable experiences that go beyond typical tourism.

What started as a small family operation has evolved into a professional team of travel experts, each bringing their unique knowledge and love for Sri Lanka to every tour we organize. We've had the privilege of welcoming thousands of travelers from around the world, each leaving with stories and memories that last a lifetime.

Today, we stand proud as specialists in creating tailor-made experiences that perfectly align with our clients' dreams and expectations. Our reputation is built on trust, quality, and an unwavering commitment to excellence.

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional travel experiences that showcase the true essence of Sri Lanka, combining professional service, local expertise, and genuine hospitality to create unforgettable journeys for every traveler.

Our Vision

To be Sri Lanka's leading travel company, recognized globally for innovation, sustainability, and customer satisfaction, while promoting responsible tourism that benefits local communities and preserves our natural heritage.

What We Offer

Customized Tour Packages

Every traveler is unique, and so should be their journey. We specialize in creating tailor-made itineraries that match your specific interests, whether you're seeking wildlife adventures, cultural immersion, romantic escapes, or family fun. Our team invests time understanding your preferences to craft the perfect Sri Lankan experience.

  • Flexible durations from 3 to 21 days
  • All accommodation levels (budget to luxury)
  • Special interest tours (photography, wellness, cuisine)
  • Multi-country combinations available

Transportation Services

Reliable, comfortable, and safe transportation is crucial for a great travel experience. Our modern fleet of well-maintained vehicles and professional drivers ensure smooth journeys across Sri Lanka. From airport transfers to full-tour transportation, we've got you covered.

  • Airport pickup and drop-off services
  • Air-conditioned vehicles (cars, vans, buses)
  • Experienced English-speaking chauffeurs
  • Daily and weekly rental options

Accommodation Booking

We've partnered with the best hotels, resorts, boutique properties, and guesthouses across Sri Lanka. Whether you prefer luxury beach villas, eco-lodges in the jungle, or heritage hotels in historic cities, we'll find your perfect home away from home.
