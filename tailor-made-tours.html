Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about tailor-made tours

How far in advance should I book my tailor-made tour?

We recommend booking at least 2-3 months in advance to ensure availability of accommodations and activities, especially during peak season (December to March). However, we can also accommodate last-minute requests based on availability.

Can I make changes to my itinerary after booking?

Yes! That's the beauty of tailor-made tours. We understand that plans can change, and we'll do our best to accommodate modifications. Changes made well in advance are usually easier to manage, but we're flexible even during your trip.

What's included in the tour price?

Typically, your tour includes accommodation, private transportation with an English-speaking driver-guide, entrance fees to mentioned attractions, and some meals as specified. International flights, travel insurance, personal expenses, and meals not mentioned are usually excluded. We'll provide a detailed breakdown in your customized quote.

Do you provide travel insurance?

We strongly recommend purchasing comprehensive travel insurance before your trip. While we don't provide insurance directly, we can recommend trusted providers and guide you on what coverage to look for.

Is Sri Lanka safe for tourists?

Yes! Sri Lanka is generally very safe for tourists. The local people are warm and welcoming. We provide 24/7 support during your tour and our experienced guides ensure you're well taken care of throughout your journey.

What if I have dietary restrictions or special requirements?

Absolutely no problem! Please inform us of any dietary restrictions, allergies, mobility issues, or other special requirements in your inquiry form. We'll ensure all arrangements accommodate your needs, from restaurant selections to activity modifications.
