Adventure Tours

11 Nights - 12 Days

Adventure Tours - Adrenaline & Thrills

Thrilling Adventures Await!

Sri Lanka is an adventure lover's paradise! From the misty mountains of Ella to the rushing rapids of Kitulgala, from rock climbing to white water rafting, our Adventure Tours package is designed for thrill-seekers who want to experience Sri Lanka's wild side. Challenge yourself with hiking iconic peaks, zip-lining through rainforests, surfing world-class waves, and exploring hidden caves.

This action-packed tour combines heart-pumping activities with stunning natural beauty. Climb Little Adam's Peak at sunrise, raft down Grade 3 rapids, abseil down waterfalls, trek through tea plantations, and camp under the stars. Perfect for adventure enthusiasts, active travelers, and anyone seeking an unforgettable adrenaline rush. All activities are led by certified instructors with top-quality safety equipment provided.

Adventure Tour Highlights

Mountain Hiking & Trekking

Ella Rock, Little Adam's Peak, Knuckles Range

White Water Rafting

Grade 2-3 rapids in Kitulgala rainforest

Zip-Lining & Abseiling

Fly through canopy, rappel down waterfalls

Camping & Outdoor Activities

Wilderness camping, rock climbing, caving

01
DAY 1: AIRPORT / KITULGALA
ARRIVAL & ADVENTURE BRIEFING

Arrival at Bandaranaike International Airport, meet our adventure guide. Drive to Kitulgala (3 hours) through scenic countryside. Check-in to jungle adventure camp. Afternoon equipment briefing and safety orientation. Evening campfire with team introduction and tour overview. Dinner at camp.

  • Overnight stay: Adventure Camp/Lodge in Kitulgala
  • Meal Plan: Breakfast & Dinner (HB)
  • Travel Time: 3 hours from airport
OVERNIGHT STAY AT KITULGALA

DAY 2 : SIGIRIYA
TRADITIONAL VILLAGE TOUR WITH LUNCH

  • Overnight stay at : 2* to 5* Star Hotel with Pool
  • Day 02 Meal Plan : Breakfast & Dinner ( HB )
  • Expected Travel Time | Negombo - Sigiriya - 03 1/2 Hour drive
OVER NIGHT STAY AT SIGIRIYA
