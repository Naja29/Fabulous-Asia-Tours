About This Tour

Celebrate your love in the paradise island of Sri Lanka! Our specially crafted honeymoon package combines romance, adventure, and relaxation. From the cultural wonders of Kandy to the misty mountains of Nuwara Eliya and pristine beaches of the south coast, create unforgettable memories together.

Enjoy intimate moments with candlelit dinners, private excursions, luxury accommodations, and personalized service. Experience the world's most scenic train journey, explore ancient temples, watch elephants in their natural habitat, and relax on golden beaches. This is your perfect romantic escape!

01
DAY 1 : ARRIVAL IN PARADISE
COLOMBO TO NEGOMBO

Welcome to Sri Lanka! Meet and greet at the airport with flower garlands. Transfer to Negombo Beach, check into luxury beach resort. Evening: romantic welcome dinner by the beach with sunset views.

  • Welcome drink and flower decoration in room
  • Candlelit dinner by the beach
  • Overnight: 4*/5* Beach Resort
OVERNIGHT AT NEGOMBO BEACH RESORT
02
DAY 2 : CULTURAL HEART
NEGOMBO TO KANDY

Scenic drive to Kandy (3.5 hours). Visit Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage, watch baby elephants bathing. Afternoon: Temple of the Tooth Relic, Kandy Lake walk. Evening: traditional cultural dance show, romantic dinner at hotel.

  • Private guided tour of sacred sites
  • Romantic lake-view dinner
  • Overnight: Luxury hotel with hill views
OVERNIGHT AT KANDY
03
DAY 3 : TEA COUNTRY ROMANCE
KANDY TO NUWARA ELIYA

Morning drive through scenic tea plantations. Visit tea factory, private tea tasting session. Stop at Ramboda Falls for photos. Arrive Nuwara Eliya - "Little England". Afternoon: Victoria Park, Gregory Lake boating. Evening: cozy fireside dinner.

  • Private tea tasting experience
  • Lake boating for two
  • Colonial-style hotel with fireplace
OVERNIGHT AT NUWARA ELIYA
04
DAY 4 : SCENIC TRAIN JOURNEY
NUWARA ELIYA TO ELLA

Experience one of the world's most romantic train journeys! Travel through emerald tea plantations, misty mountains, and cascading waterfalls. Arrive Ella, check into boutique hotel. Sunset at Little Adam's Peak. Evening: private candlelit dinner.

  • Reserved seats on scenic train
  • Sunset hike to viewpoint
  • Boutique hotel with mountain views
OVERNIGHT AT ELLA
05
DAY 5 : WILDLIFE ADVENTURE
ELLA TO YALA

Morning visit to Nine Arch Bridge. Drive to Yala (3 hours). Afternoon: private jeep safari in Yala National Park - spot leopards, elephants, sloth bears. Return to luxury tented camp. Evening: bush dinner under the stars.

  • Private safari jeep
  • Luxury glamping experience
  • Starlit dinner in the wilderness
OVERNIGHT AT YALA LUXURY CAMP
06
DAY 6 : BEACH PARADISE
YALA TO MIRISSA

Transfer to Mirissa Beach (2 hours). Check into beachfront resort. Day at leisure: relax on golden sands, swim in turquoise waters, enjoy couple spa treatment. Sunset cocktails on the beach. Private beach dinner with bonfire.

  • Beachfront luxury villa
  • Couple spa session (2 hours)
  • Private beach setup with bonfire
OVERNIGHT AT MIRISSA BEACH
07
DAY 7 : COASTAL EXPLORATION
MIRISSA & GALLE

Optional whale watching tour (Nov-Apr). Morning at leisure on beach. Afternoon: visit historic Galle Fort (UNESCO), walk the ramparts, explore boutique shops and cafes. Return to Mirissa. Evening: farewell dinner by the ocean.

  • Optional whale watching
  • Galle Fort guided tour
  • Romantic sunset dinner
OVERNIGHT AT MIRISSA BEACH
08
DAY 8 : DEPARTURE
MIRISSA TO AIRPORT

Leisure morning at beach. Late checkout. Transfer to airport (2.5 hours). Depart with beautiful memories of your Sri Lankan honeymoon!

  • Breakfast & late checkout
  • Airport transfer
  • Complimentary photo album
